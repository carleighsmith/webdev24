In this chapter on debugging, I used two primary methods to trace my errors. Firstly, I employed window.alerts to trace my errors. Secondly, I utilized breakpoints within my browser's developer tools. By adding breakpoints at critical points in my code, the runtime is paused at the points and examined. This identifies unexpected behaviors.
