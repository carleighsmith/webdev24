How to Prepare for an Interview

Research shows that preparing for an interview can increase your chances of landing the job. Here are some tips to help you prepare:

1. Research the company and position
2. Review common interview questions
3. Dress appropriately
4. Practice your answers
5. Bring a copy of your resume and references
